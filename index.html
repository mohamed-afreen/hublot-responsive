HUBLOT POLO GOLD CUP

After two years of interruption caused by the pandemic, Europe's highest altitude tournament – the Hublot Polo Gold Cup Gstaad – made its comeback from 18 to 21 August 2022, to the great delight of its spectators! To the rhythm of hooves and mallets, officially timed by Hublot, the closely-fought chukkers were played, with Team Clinique La Prairie claiming victory as the winner of this 25th edition.
